Your Privacy is Sacred

Mental health data is deeply personal. We've built MindGuard from the ground up with privacy-first architecture that puts you in complete control.

  • End-to-end encryption for all voice recordings
  • Local processing - your data stays on your device
  • Zero-knowledge architecture - we can't access your raw data
  • Granular consent controls for every feature
  • HIPAA compliant infrastructure and processes
  • Complete data portability and deletion rights
